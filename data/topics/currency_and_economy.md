# Currency types, trading conventions, league economics, and vendor recipes

## Core Trading Currencies

| Currency | Function | Typical Value | Notes |
|----------|----------|---------------|-------|
| Chaos Orb | Rerolls rare item mods | 1c (base unit) | Universal price reference — all values expressed in "c" |
| Divine Orb | Rerolls numeric values of existing mods | 180-250c mid-league | Premium trade currency; very expensive at league start (300-500c+) |
| Exalted Orb | Adds random mod to rare (if open affix) | 10-50c (varies) | Less trade-relevant since Divine Orb prominence; still used in crafting |
| Mirror of Kalandra | Creates perfect copy (mirrored = unmodifiable) | Hundreds of divines | Rarest currency; used only on "mirror-tier" perfect items |

> **Pricing Formats:** "5c" = 5 chaos, "2div" = 2 divines, "1div 50c" = mixed. Fractions like "0.5div" = half a divine (90-125c depending on rate).

## Crafting Currencies

### Upgrade Path Currencies

| Currency | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| Transmutation Orb | Normal → Magic (1-2 mods). Lowest tier, used for map/flask rolling |
| Orb of Alchemy | Normal → Rare (random mods). Essential for map progression |
| Regal Orb | Magic → Rare (adds one mod). Key step in alt-regal crafting |

### Modification Currencies

| Currency | Effect | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Orb of Alteration | Rerolls magic item mods | High-volume base crafting for flasks, jewels, maps |
| Orb of Augmentation | Adds mod to magic item (if space) | Pairs with alterations |
| Annulment Orb | Removes random mod from magic/rare | High-risk; valuable for advanced crafting (10-15c) |
| Orb of Scouring | Removes all mods (item becomes normal) | Crafting reset; often paired with alchemy orbs |
| Orb of Regret | Refunds one passive skill point | 2-4c typical |

### Socket & Link Currencies

| Currency | Effect | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Chromatic Orb | Rerolls socket colors | Vendor recipe: item with R-G-B linked sockets |
| Jeweller's Orb | Rerolls number of sockets | Vendor recipe: 6-socket item → 7 jeweller's |
| Orb of Fusing | Rerolls links between sockets | ~1000 average for 6-link; major build expense |

### High-Risk Currencies

| Currency | Effect | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Vaal Orb | Corrupts item unpredictably | Can add powerful implicits, change gem levels, or brick the item entirely |
| Ancient Orb | Rerolls unique into different unique of same base | Niche use chasing specific uniques |

> **"Vaal or no balls"** — Vaal Orbs are the ultimate gamble. Corruption is irreversible and can improve, destroy, or fundamentally change an item.

## Fragment Currencies

### Breach

100 splinters = 1 breachstone → boss encounter. Five factions: Xoph (fire), Tul (cold), Esh (lightning), Uul-Netol (physical), Chayula (chaos). Chayula typically most valuable.

### Legion

Similar splinter→emblem system. 5-way Legion encounters are very profitable but require group coordination. Factions: Maraketh, Karui, Eternal Empire, Templar, Vaal.

### Map Fragments

- **Sacrifice/Mortal fragments:** Open Atziri encounters
- **Shaper/Elder fragments:** Access endgame boss encounters
- All fragments add quantity/rarity bonuses when placed in the map device

## League Economics

### League Start (Weeks 1-2)

Chaos is scarce, everything costs more in chaos terms. Divine Orbs spike to 300-500c+. Basic currencies (chromatics, fusings, jeweller's) trade at a premium. Meta uniques are expensive due to high demand and low supply. Raw currency drops are worth more than usual relative to items.

### Mid-League Stabilization (Weeks 3-8)

Currency ratios settle to historical norms. Divines reach 180-250c. Increased supply drives most prices down. Crafting becomes accessible for average players.

### Late League (Weeks 9+)

Currency is abundant, most items are cheap. Market shifts focus to perfect/mirror-tier items. Standard league often receives currency dumps from migrating characters.

### Cross-League Context

- **Standard:** Permanent league; items accumulate forever, prices reflect years of supply
- **Challenge leagues:** Temporary (3-4 months), characters/items migrate to Standard at end
- **Hardcore:** Death sends character + inventory to softcore equivalent
- Most active trading occurs in the current challenge league

## Trading Conventions

### Bulk Trading

Currency exchange operates like a market — buy one type for another. Exchange rates fluctuate on supply/demand. Premiums apply for convenience and larger quantities. Minimum stock requirements are common for bulk listings.

### Value Assessment

- **poe.ninja:** Real-time market prices and trend graphs — the primary price reference
- **Chaos equivalent:** Standard comparison metric across all currency types
- **Historical context:** Compare current prices to previous league values for trend analysis
- **Meta dependency:** Skill/item popularity directly drives demand and price

## Advanced Economic Concepts

**Currency Flipping:** Buying currency at low rates, selling at higher rates. Profitable but time-intensive; requires market knowledge and timing.

**Crafting Economics:** Expected value calculations determine whether to craft or buy finished items. Understanding modifier probabilities and average attempt costs is essential for profitable crafting.

**Inflation Patterns:** Early league sees high inflation as currency accumulates; mid-league stabilizes; late league deflates as players quit.

**Market Manipulation:** Price fixing (coordinated price control) exists. Always cross-reference multiple sources and be suspicious of prices significantly below market rate with no actual trades completing.

## Vendor Recipes

### Currency Generation

| Recipe | Input | Output |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Chromatic Orb | Item with R-G-B linked sockets | 1 Chromatic |
| Jeweller's Orb | 6-socket item | 7 Jeweller's |
| Divine Orb | 6-linked item (including corrupted) | 1 Divine |
| Chaos Orb | Full rare set (helm, chest, gloves, boots, belt, 2 rings, amulet, weapon), all ilvl 60-74 | 1-2 Chaos |
| Regal Orb | Same full rare set, all ilvl 75+ | 1 Regal |

### Quality Recipes

Vendor items of the same type totaling 40% quality → 1 quality orb of that type (e.g., 40% total quality flasks → 1 Glassblower's Bauble).

## Budget Tiers

| Tier | Budget | Focus |
|------|--------|-------|
| League Start | 0-10 div | Life/resistances, basic damage. Rares often beat budget uniques. Prioritize 5-link. |
| Mid-Range | 10-50 div | Meta uniques, 6-links, real weapon upgrades, targeted crafting projects |
| High-End | 50+ div | Near-perfect items, multiple expensive uniques, advanced crafting, secondary stat min-maxing |
| Mirror-Tier | 100s of div | Perfect rares, legacy Standard items, absolute min-max on every slot |
