# 3.28.0 Mirage league — balance changes, atlas rework, new gems, Harbinger removed

## League Mechanic: Mirage

- Fight Afarud Warlocks holding trapped Djinn, then enter The Astral Realm (a mirage copy of the surrounding area)
- Choose 1 of 3 **Wishes** before entering — modifies the Mirage to drop specific loot types (currency, gear, caches, mysterious coins)
- Clear monsters, astral hazards, and Afarud mages to reach the tether and free the Djinn
- Standard/HC/SSF variants available; HC deaths move to Standard Mirage
- 40 new challenges; Orb Regalia Armour Set at 12/20/28/36 completions

## New Skill Gems

### Active Skills (Str/Int)
- **Divine Blast:** Shield beam → implosion → delayed larger explosion
- **Holy Hammers:** Ground slam calls cascading hammers forward; expend Power Charge for side hammers (mace/staff)
- **Holy Strike:** Melee hit spawns permanent Holy Armament minions that copy main-hand weapon stats (mace/sceptre/staff)
- **Shield of Light:** Block retaliation — sweeps shield in cone, allies near first target emit additional waves
- **Holy Sweep** (reworked Sweep): Two-handed mace/staff circle swing, calls Holy Hammers on random enemies hit (unevadable)

### New Transfigured Gems
- Shockwave Totem of Authority, Static Strike of Gathering Lightning, Charged Dash of Projection, Flamethrower Trap of Stability, Kinetic Fusillade of Detonation, Orb of Storms of Squalls, Shock Nova of Procession, Storm Burst of Repulsion, Siphoning Trap of Pain

### New Support Gems
- **Blessed Call Support** (Str/Int): Warcries create Consecrated Ground (lower AoE/cooldown)
- **Excommunicate** (Str/Int): Melee attacks gain phys→fire+lightning extra damage, excommunicate on hit
- **Exemplar Support** (Int): Minions gain crit chance/multi per your recent crits
- **Hallow** (Str/Int): Self-used melee attacks inflict Hallowing Flame on hit

## New Scion Ascendancy: Reliquarian

- Notables borrow powers from Unique items found throughout Wraeclast
- Notables change each league — not available from Forbidden Flame/Flesh jewels

## Exceptional Support Gems

- New tier replacing Awakened Support Gems (which are being phased out)
- 40+ Exceptional Supports, found in endgame
- Awakened Empower/Enlighten/Enhance still obtainable; all other Awakened gems removed from drop pool
- New Coins (Knowledge/Power/Skill) corrupt level 20 gems, imbuing with random Int/Str/Dex support effect

## Major Endgame & Atlas Rework

### Map System Overhaul
- **Maps are no longer area-specific** — a "Tier 10 Map" item works for any T10 area; pick the area on your Atlas when activating
- Atlas starts from center; 4 corners house Voidstone slots
- All content (including Unique Maps) physically visible on Atlas as fog clears
- Favoured Map system removed (no longer needed)
- Cartographer's Chisels removed from drop pool; existing still work but at reduced values (20% quality = +10% IQ, down from +20%)
- All Maven's Chisels halved in effect
- Map Device crafting options removed

### Astrolabes (New Atlas Mechanic)
- Using an Astrolabe on a map creates a **Shaped Region** in that Atlas quadrant
- Complete content in Shaped Region maps to clear influence; remaining maps gain extra mods
- Clearing a full Shaped Region opens a **Memory Vault** (quadrant-specific rewards)

### Voidstone Changes
- Decayed Voidstone: kill both Shaper AND Elder (or Uber Elder)
- Exarch/Eater Voidstone: kill both Searing Exarch AND Eater of Worlds (combined, not separate)
- New Voidstone: kill Incarnation of Dread
- New Memory fragments: Memory of Loneliness→Incarnation of Neglect, Memory of Trauma→Incarnation of Fear, Memory of Reverence→Incarnation of Dread
- After Dread Voidstone obtained, Memory Strand items drop from all maps

### Nightmare Maps (T17 Rework)
- T17s renamed to Nightmare Maps with significant changes
- Each completion awards 1 Atlas Skill Point (5 total)

### Other Endgame
- Uber Bosses now require 4 fragments (down from 5)
- Unique Maps grant 1 Atlas Skill Point each (up to 10; ~20 available, pick your half)
- All Maven Invitations accessed through Kirac
- Sacrifice/Mortal Fragments now used alongside maps (add Vaal Side Area + IQ bonus), no longer open areas directly
- Blessed Orbs reroll Conqueror/Elder Guardian type on their maps
- Kirac Missions removed entirely; related Atlas Passives removed
- Atlas Bonus Objectives now also grant +1% Map IQ per 4 completions

## Significant Skill Gem Balance

### Buffs
- **Storm Brand:** Massive damage buff — base damage ~+60%, effectiveness 45-55% (from 30%)
- **Penance Brand:** +10% more damage all levels, effectiveness 520-630% (from 470-570%), no longer 30% less ailment damage
- **Absolution:** Debuff 3s (from 1s), minion duration 15s (from 10s), sentinel 44% more AoE, blast 40% more damage
- **Dominating Blow:** Sentinel duration 30s (from 20s), debuff 3s (from 1s), normal sentinel proc on rare hits
- **Static Strike:** 2.5m range (from 2), 4s duration (from 3s), 30% less beam damage (from 40%)
- **Consecrated Path:** Effectiveness 273-468% (from 227-417%)
- **Purifying Flame of Revelations:** Lower mana cost, faster cast, shockwave 150% more (from 110%)
- **Raised Spectres:** Better scaling past gem level 20 (level 85 at gem 30, from 81); no hidden ele bonus on conversion
- **Holy Flame Totem:** Now fires 3-6 projectiles (from flat 3)
- **Summon Holy Relic:** Nova heals minions for 9-985 life/s (from 9-464)
- **Vaal Domination/Absolution:** Ascended sentinels 300% more damage (from 200%)

### Nerfs
- **Detonate Dead of Scavenging:** 70% less ailment damage (from 60%)
- **Earthshatter:** Effectiveness reduced
- **Viper Strike of the Mamba:** 30% less attack speed dual wield, cannot multi-poison per hit
- **Overexertion Support:** 7-10% more per warcry (from 12-18%)
- **Autoexertion:** 20% reservation (from 15%)

### Other
- Intelligence now grants 1% increased max ES per 10 Int (from per 5) — massive ES nerf
- Greater supports no longer stack with non-Greater version (e.g., GMP + LMP no longer works)
- Lesser Multiple Projectiles renamed to Multiple Projectiles
- Orb of Storms can now be supported by Spell Echo
- All reflected damage reduction → "cannot be Reflected" percentage
