# 3.27.0 Keepers of the Flame — Breach rework league, 10 new Bloodline ascendancies, asynchronous trading, new wand gems

## League Mechanic: Keepers of the Flame (Breach Rework)

- Breach Hives and Unstable Breaches infest areas; protect Ailith as she empowers the Flame to destroy them
- Unstable Breaches open via massive Breach Hands — kill monsters fast enough to stabilise the Breach
- The Monastery of the Keepers houses the **Genesis Tree**, which grows twisted rewards: currency, uniques, equipment, and **Grafts** (breach-hand items fused directly to your body)
- Standard/HC/SSF variants available; HC deaths move to Standard Keepers
- 40 new challenges; Keeper of the Flame Armour Set at 12/20/28/36 completions

## New Content & Features

### New Skill Gems

- **Conflagration** (Dex): Fire arrows into the air, raining down in targeted area; moving near stuck arrows causes chain explosions with fire DoT debuff
- **Thunderstorm** (Dex): Arrow lands at target, creates blinding thunderstorm that gains stages while you stand in it; leaving at max stages triggers Thunderburst explosion. Cannot be used by totems/traps/mines
- **Somatic Shell** (Int): Projectile applies kinetic barrier that absorbs damage up to a cap, then explodes and chains to nearby targets
- **Kinetic Fusillade** (Int): Manifest hovering Anomalies that fling at enemies after a delay, chaining on impact; attacking resets duration for all active Anomalies. Cannot be used with traps/mines
- **Kinetic Rain** (Int): Wand swing rains projectiles targeting enemy locations in a large area, each triggering Kinetic Anomaly. Cannot be triggered or used with traps/mines/totems
- **Kinetic Rain of Impact** (Int, Transfigured): Same as Kinetic Rain but without Kinetic Anomaly trigger
- **Wall of Force** (Int): Creates a wall that allied Chain projectiles collide with, Chaining off to nearby targets

### New Support Gems

- **Windburst** (Dex): Attack skills trigger Windburst on hit after travelling a certain distance
- **Living Lightning** (Int): Damaging hit skills trigger Summon Living Lightning when dealing lightning damage. Cannot support totem/trap/mine skills

### New Items & Currency

- 20+ new Unique items, 5 new Divination Cards
- 3 new Wand base types: **Blasting Wand, Kinetic Wand, Somatic Wand** (implicit: Cannot roll Caster Modifiers)
- 6 new Ring base types (Keepers league): Cryonic, Enthalpic, Formless, Fugitive, Organic, Synaptic
- **Foulborn Orb of Augmentation/Regal/Exalted** — crafting orbs biased toward higher-level modifiers
- **Augmentation/Regal/Exalted/Unstable Implant** — crafting currency for Grafts
- Vaal Orbs now function as Remnants of Corruption on Essence monsters; Remnants removed from game

### Bloodlines (New System)

- Secondary Ascendancy Classes unlocked per-character by defeating specific endgame bosses
- 10 Bloodline Classes; pair with any Ascendancy Class using normal Ascendancy points
- Allocate points freely between original Ascendancy and Bloodline

### Asynchronous Trading

- **Faustus** NPC in Lioneye's Watch (Act 6) stewards Merchant's Tabs for offline direct-purchase trading
- In-game Trade Market interface added (press `,` to open)
- Premium Stash Tabs purchased before PoE2 Third Edict can be converted via website tool

### Other Notable Features

- NPC bulk identification in each town (no Scrolls of Wisdom required); Wisdom Scroll drops vastly reduced after Act 1
- Spectres on death become untargetable **Orb Spectres** that auto-revive on entering new areas
- Hold Right-click + Ctrl on Stacked Decks to continually open them; add Shift to drop cards to ground
- Full passive tree refund available for Gold (one click); Atlas passive refund via Gold at Kirac
- Bestiary Orbs removed; itemise beasts directly from Menagerie UI

## Endgame Changes

## League Changes

- Breach Blessings no longer upgrade uniques — now only upgrade Breachstones
- Breach removed as reward outcome from other content; Valdo's Puzzle Box cannot produce Breach outcomes
- Vaal Breach unobtainable during Keepers league
- Heist Breach reward rooms converted to **Gold reward rooms**
- Heist Rogues now start at maximum level for proficient jobs
- Trarthan Gems now found in Heist Gem side-chests
- Opening a Blight chest now opens other Blight chests within 1.5m
- Betrayal: characters must have entered Act 9 Highgate to open Safehouses; Syndicate Medallions more common from high-level Safehouses; fewer high-rarity Div Cards from Gravicius Transportation
- Harvest reforge crafts now respect "Cannot Roll X Mods" restrictions
- Kingsmarch: reroll cost reduced to 16000 Gold (from 20000); workers with Mapping/Disenchanting/Farming expertise less likely to also have other job types
- Rogue Exiles drop fewer regular items (bonus equipment drops unchanged)

## Player Changes

- **Spell Suppression** now prevents 40% of damage from suppressed hits and ailments (previously 50%)
- **Minion attacks now inherently always hit** — all sources of Minion Accuracy removed from game
- Drowning Orbs no longer drain faster with "Debuffs expire 100% faster"
